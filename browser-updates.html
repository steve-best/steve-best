Thoughts on Browser Updates

I came across an interesting article over on Arstechnica earlier this week, and it got me thinking about the way that Apple updates Safari on iOS. Because Safari is a part of the OS and not separated from the OS itself, Apple updates Safari through updates to iOS itself. It seems to me that a more efficient method to update Safari would be to decouple it from the OS so that it can be updated through the App Store. This is how Google does it with Chrome on Android, and quite frankly, I think it's a much better approach.
