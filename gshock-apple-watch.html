G-Shock Style Apple Watch

September 23, 2021

My beautiful wife knows that I like Casio's G-Shock watches, and I wore the same G-Shock as my daily watch for about five years prior to getting my Apple Watch in 2018. One of the gifts my wife bought for me for my birthday this year was a watch band that gives the Apple Watch that “G-Shock” look. I like it alot. The band is one piece, and the watch itself fits inside it, almost like a cell phone case. The rubbery plastic material seems to be of good quality so far, and I think it really does a great job of mimicking that G-Shock look and feel. So much so in fact that when I went to pay with my watch at the grocery store yesterday, the kid working the check out goes, “Dude, I didn't know you could pay for stuff with a G-Shock?!?”. 🤓
