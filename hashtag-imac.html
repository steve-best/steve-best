#iMac.

I have an old iMac that my wife and I bought new in 2008. We used it as our “family computer” for a number of years. It can no longer run macOS, and Apple has long since stopped supporting it. However, it still sits on the desk in my home office beside my work computer, and I use it just about every day. I think Macs of this vintage are of remarkable quality, and I love the design. It still has the original HDD, and I have only upgraded the RAM. I plan to keep using it until it ceases to function.
