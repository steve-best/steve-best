Hi, I'm Steve.

I'm a Charlotte, NC native and a follower of Christ. I am blessed with a beautiful wife, a college sophomore, a senior in high school, and a fifth grader. I'm a PCI ISA, a PCIP, a security enthusiast, a Linux enthusiast, and an all-around tech nerd. I also love the game of basketball and Japanese cars.

I used to have an “Art Directed Journal”, and like my former site, the premise of this blog is still “art directing” each post. Although art directed blog posts are not as trendy as they once were, I'm mainly using this site as an excuse to practice HTML and CSS, as well as hone my writing skills. Additionally, I find this blogging format to be quite enjoyable because it scratches my itch to design. As you can probably tell, since I've neglected to buy a proper domain, this site is hosted on Github Pages. Topics will include whatever strikes my interest, but mainly I like to stick with topics related to my personal life, technology and productivity.
